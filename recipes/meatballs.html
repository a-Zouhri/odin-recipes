Meatballs

Description

Create delectable meatballs by combining ground beef, breadcrumbs, onions, garlic, and a blend of herbs and spices. Shape the mixture into meatballs and cook until they are golden brown and juicy. Savor the flavorful homemade meatballs as a delightful and satisfying dish.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Onions, finely chopped
  • Garlic, minced
  • Herbs and spices (such as parsley, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper)
  • Olive oil
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese, shredded

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine ground beef, breadcrumbs, chopped onions, minced garlic, and your preferred herbs and spices. Mix well to incorporate all the ingredients.
  3. Shape the meat mixture into bite-sized meatballs and place them on a greased baking sheet.
  4. Drizzle the meatballs with olive oil to enhance browning and moisture.
  5. Bake the meatballs in the preheated oven for about 20-25 minutes, or until they are cooked through and browned.
  6. In a separate saucepan, heat tomato sauce over medium heat until warmed through.
  7. Once the meatballs are cooked, remove them from the oven and transfer them to the simmering tomato sauce.
  8. Gently stir the meatballs in the sauce to coat them evenly, allowing them to simmer for a few minutes.
  9. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the meatballs and sauce.
  10. Cover the saucepan with a lid and let the cheese melt for a few minutes, until it becomes gooey and bubbly.
  11. Serve the meatballs with the tomato sauce and melted cheese as a main dish or as a filling for sandwiches or subs.
