Mojito

Description

Quench your thirst with a refreshing and zesty mojito cocktail. This classic drink combines the flavors of fresh mint, tangy lime, and a hint of sweetness. It's the perfect beverage to enjoy on a sunny day or to liven up any social gathering.

Ingredients

  • White rum
  • Fresh mint leaves
  • Lime
  • Simple syrup
  • Soda water
  • Ice cubes

Steps

  1. In a sturdy glass, muddle a handful of fresh mint leaves with the juice of half a lime. The muddling process releases the mint's aromatic oils.
  2. Add 2 ounces of white rum to the glass. Adjust the amount based on your preference for a stronger or milder drink.
  3. Add 1 ounce of simple syrup to sweeten the cocktail. You can adjust the amount to suit your taste.
  4. Squeeze the juice of the other half of the lime into the glass.
  5. Fill the glass with ice cubes to the brim.
  6. Top off the glass with soda water, leaving some space for stirring.
  7. Use a spoon or a cocktail stirrer to gently mix the ingredients together, ensuring the flavors are evenly distributed.
  8. Garnish the cocktail with a sprig of fresh mint and a slice of lime.
  9. Serve the mojito immediately and enjoy its invigorating flavors.
